About

Building the Systems Behind Financial Safety

I am a Fintech AI Engineer based in Ibadan, Nigeria, specialising in financial crime detection and risk management infrastructure. I build the systems that run silently behind every card swipe, every bank transfer, every account opening — deciding in milliseconds whether something is legitimate or fraud.

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, event-driven architecture, and financial compliance. I understand not just how to build these systems, but why they are designed the way they are — the regulatory requirements, the operational trade-offs, and what happens when they get things wrong.

I am open to remote roles, internships, and collaboration opportunities in fraud engineering, risk scoring, and AML compliance systems.
